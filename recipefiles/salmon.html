5 Ingredient Salmon

Description

This easy salmon recipe is a delicious way to prepare fresh fillets using just a few ingredients from your pantry.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp garlic powder

  • 1 tbsp dried basil

  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • 4 (6 oz) fillets salmon

  • 2 tbsp butter

  • 4 lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Stir garlic powder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl.

  3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.

  4. Serve salmon with lemon wedges.
