Bacon Alfredo Tortellini

Description

Need something incredibly good and a bit on the decadent side? This bacon Alfredo tortellini checks all the boxes. It's rich, creamy, comforting, and very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 3 slices bacon

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 teaspoon bouillon, or 1/4 cup chicken stock

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

  • 3 ounces cream cheese, cut into chunks

  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C). Line a sheet pan with a silicon mat. Lay the bacon on the sheet pan.

  2. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp, turning once, about 30 minutes. Set aside on paper towels to drain. Reserve about 1 tablespoon bacon grease.

  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook tortellini according to package directions. Drain and set aside.

  4. Bring the pot back to the stove. Over medium heat, combine garlic, bouillon, cream, cream cheese, and bacon grease. Stir and cook until all melted and thickened, about 5 minutes. Add about half the cheese, and season to taste with salt and pepper.

  5. Stir in tortellini. Crumble bacon and add to the pot. Stir to combine. Serve in wide bowls with remaining Parmesan; garnish with parsley.
